    About the application

    Symptoms of Covid-19

  • What are the most common covid-19 symptoms?

    The most common symptoms are listed here:

    1. Fever
    2. Cough
    3. Myalgia (aching muscles)
    4. Sputum production
    5. Hemoptysis (coughing blood)
    6. Diarrhea
    7. Imaparement of Smell
    8. Imparement of Taste

  • How do I know wheter I have covid-19 or the newly mutated genome of the virus?

    This has to be determined by a test from a certified testing facility.

  • I have many of the symptoms, does that mean I have covid? What should I do?

    The presence of several symptoms alone is not guarantee for having covid-19. However, a doctor should be contacted as soon as possible. Also observe the develppment of the symptoms and wether the condition is worsening or improving over time. To do so, please use the symptom tracker and share the results width your doctor.

  • When should I contact a doctor?

    Contact a doctor as soon as:

    1. You have high fever or rising temperature
    2. You experience hemoptysis
    3. Your sense of taste is significantly impared
    4. Your sense of smell is significantly impared
    5. You have many of the listed covid-19 symptoms
    6. You see from the symptom tracker that your symptoms and overall condition are worsening over time

    Treatment

  • My symptoms are getting worse, how can I get treated?

    The symptom tracker is not yet connected to a hospital or medical staff. Please contact the closest hospital or doctor.

  • Which is the closest hospital in my region?

    As the symptom tracker is not yet connected to all doctors and hospitals you have to lool for the most convenient treatment center yourself.

  • How can I treat myself if I don't have acess to a hospital?

    Use the symptom tracker to closely oberve the bevaviour and developpment of your symptoms and try taking measures accordingly. Also contact a doctor for further advice and share your data from the symptom tracker with your doctor.

    Symptom tracker

  • How do I use the tracker to record and dokument the developpment of my symptoms?

    Use the tracker every day to log your symptoms. Fill in the form then submit the information. When information about your symptoms is submitted use the track funtion to display the developpment of your symptoms over time. You will see two graphs. One graph displays the logs of your temperature which is labelled as "Fever". The second graph will depict the developpment over time of all other symptoms on a scale from 1 to 10. For better readability click on one of the symptoms to disable it from the grapgh.
