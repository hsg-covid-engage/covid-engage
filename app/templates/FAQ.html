    About the application

  • What is the purpose of the Covid-Engage project?

    The Covid-Engange project was launched by three students from the University of St. Gallen. The application was built during the Course "Software Engineering for Economists". Its purpose is the tracking of covid symptoms of its users.

  • Will this application ever be used in real life?

    The application is still under development. Once the Platform is fully devlopend and deemed to be useful, it will be deployed in real life.

  • Who created this application?

    The creators are: Bryan Perry Gyger, Mario Blauensteiner, Tobias Klauser and Philip Arpad Gerber

    Privacy

  • Do I have full control over my data?

    Off course the Covid-Engage Team will delete all data if this is requested by a user. However, the deletion of data cannot be undertaken by the user directly.

  • How long is my data stored?

    After the publication of the final grade for the coursework all stored data will be deleted. This is expected to happen before March 2021.

  • Who has access to my data?

    Only the creators of this project and the professor grading the Covid-Engage project have access to the database.

    Symptoms of Covid-19

  • What are the most common covid-19 symptoms?

    The most common symptoms are listed here:

    1. Fever
    2. Cough
    3. Myalgia (aching muscles)
    4. Sputum production
    5. Hemoptysis (coughing blood)
    6. Diarrhea
    7. Imapairement of Smell
    8. Impairement of Taste

  • How do I know whether I have covid-19 or the newly mutated genome of the virus?

    This has to be determined by a test from a certified testing facility.

  • I have many of the symptoms, does that mean I have covid? What should I do?

    In general this application is designed for covid-positive people as a tool to surveil the development of the illness. The presence of several symptoms alone is not guarantee for having covid.

  • When should I contact a doctor?

    Contact a doctor as soon as:

    1. You have high fever or rising temperature
    2. You experience hemoptysis
    3. Your sense of taste is significantly impaired
    4. Your sense of smell is significantly impaired
    5. You have many of the listed covid symptoms
    6. You see from the symptom tracker that your symptoms and overall condition are worsening over time

    Treatment

  • My symptoms are getting worse, how can I get treated?

    The symptom tracker is not yet connected to a hospital or medical staff. Please contact the closest hospital or doctor.

  • Which is the closest hospital in my region?

    As the symptom tracker is not yet connected to all doctors and hospitals you have to tool for the most convenient treatment centre yourself.

  • How can I treat myself if I don't have access to a hospital?

    Use the symptom tracker to closely observe the behaviour and development of your symptoms and try taking measures accordingly. Also contact a doctor for further advice and share your data from the symptom tracker with your doctor.

    Symptom tracker

  • How do I use the tracker to record and document the development of my symptoms?

    Use the tracker every day to log your symptoms. Fill in the form then submit the information. Indicate your body temperature (in degrees Celcius) using the drop-down called "Fever". Indicate your other symptoms using the respective drop-down tabs where you are asked to state the severity on a scale from 1 to 10 (10 being the maximum). When information about your symptoms is submitted use the track function to display the development of your symptoms over time. You will see two graphs. One graph displays the logs of your temperature which is labelled as "Fever". The second graph will depict the development over time of all other symptoms on a scale from 1 to 10. For better readability click on one of the symptoms to disable it from the graph.

    Account

  • What is meant by "Physical Health" in the registration form?

    It is a self assed state of your own perceived level physical health. Please indicate how "fit" you consider yourself. A self-assessment should be honest and realistic in order to provide useful information.

  • Will my profile automatically be deleted after some time?

    Your profile will be deleted after the authors of this web-application have received their final grade.
